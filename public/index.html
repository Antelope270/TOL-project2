Calculate the compound interest rate

Task 1

Suppose you deposit $3,000 at 12% per year compounded per month. How much will be paid from your account after 8 months?

Suggested answer

Interest rate per year “r” is 12%, compounded times per year “m” is 12; As a result, the interest rates i = r / m = 12% / 12 = 1%. Combined with the Initial principal “p” is 3000, we can calculate the principal amount A(n) = P(1+i)^n = 3000*(1+1%)^8 = 3248.5

Task 2

Suppose you deposit $1,000 at 6% per year compounded per month. How much will be paid from your account after 3 months?

Suggested answer

Interest rate per year “r” is 6%, compounded times per year “m” is 12; As a result, the interest rates i = r/m = 6%/12 = 0.5%. Combined with the Initial principal “p” is 1000, we can calculate the principal amount A(n) = P(1+i)^n = 1000*(1+0.5%)^3 = 1015.1
